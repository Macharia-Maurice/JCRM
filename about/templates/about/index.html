ABOUT JCRM

JCRM (Jesus Christ Revival Movement) was born on August 15, 2015, and started in a two-bedroom apartment in the city of Lagos, Nigeria. It was founded by Pastor David and Pastor Mary with a vision to ignite faith and transform lives through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Over the years, JCRM has grown into a vibrant community of believers who are passionate about experiencing God's power, embracing His love, and walking in His grace. Our mission is to reach out to the lost, the broken, and the hurting with the message of hope and restoration.

We believe that through the power of the Holy Spirit, lives can be transformed, and destinies can be fulfilled. Join us on this journey of faith as we seek to know God more and make Him known to the world.

OUR MISSION & VISION

At JCRM, our mission is to ignite a deep relationship with Jesus Christ, empower believers to live out their faith, and transform lives through the power of the Holy Spirit. Our vision is to see a world where every person experiences the love of God, walks in His grace, and fulfills their God-given purpose.

We are committed to reaching out to the lost, the broken, and the hurting with the message of hope and restoration. Through our various ministries, we aim to create an environment where people can encounter God, grow in their faith, and be equipped to impact their world for Christ.
